About the Library

The Central Library of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering is a hub of knowledge and learning, offering a wide collection of books, journals, e-resources, and research materials.

Established with a mission to support academic and research needs, the library provides students and faculty with access to national and international resources in science, engineering, technology, and management.

The library features:

  • Thousands of printed books and reference materials
  • Digital Library with online journals and databases
  • Reading areas and discussion zones
  • Wi-Fi enabled study environment
  • Library automation with barcode systems

The Central Library encourages students to explore, research, and innovate by giving access to the right information at the right time.
